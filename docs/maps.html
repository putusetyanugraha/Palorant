MAPS

Explore the battlefields where champions are made

LITUS

Beneath a forgotten monastery, a clandestine facility in LITUS is divided by three sites. Exotic mechanics and verticality define this map, creating unique gameplay scenarios and strategic opportunities. Teams must adapt their tactics to control these areas effectively.

-- 3 Spike Sites
-- 6 Main Entrances
-- Multiple Elevations

FRANCER

Two sites. No middle. Seaside morocco is the setting for FRANCER, featuring one-way teleporters that allow quick rotation through the map. Attackers can use the teleporters to outmaneuver and surprise defenders, but teleporting creates distinct audio cues that alert nearby players.

-- 2 Spike Sites
-- One-Way Teleporters
-- Tight Corners

BOND

An urban downtown area split in two by radianite technology. Teams must navigate this vertical battleground carefully, as height advantages can quickly change the tide of battle. Rope ascenders allow quick vertical repositioning, creating dynamic gameplay opportunities.

-- 2 Spike Sites
-- Vertical Combat
-- Rope Ascenders

PEAR

An open playground for small wars of position and attrition. PEAR features a large, open middle area that both teams can skirmish over. The middle area divides the map and provides quick access to both sites, making controlling this space crucial for success.

-- 2 Spike Sites
-- Mechanical Doors
-- Open Mid Area

GLUT

A secret Giot excavation site overtaken by the arctic. GLUT features complex verticality and ziplines that create unique combat situations. Teams must adapt to cramped spaces, with multiple angles to cover and sightlines to watch for maximum tactical advantage.

-- 2 Spike Sites
-- Ziplines
-- Verticality & Angles
