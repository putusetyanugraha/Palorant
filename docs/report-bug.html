FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When will my bug be fixed?

Bug fixes are prioritized based on severity and impact. Critical bugs affecting gameplay may be addressed in hotfixes, while less severe issues are typically included in regular patch updates.

Will I get a response about my bug report?

Due to high volume, we cannot respond to every report individually. However, if we need more information about your bug, our team will contact you using the email provided.

Can I report suspicious players here?

No, this form is specifically for reporting technical issues with the game. To report players for cheating or toxic behavior, please use the in-game reporting tool.
