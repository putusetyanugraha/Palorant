ABOUT PALORANT

Palorant is a 5v5 character-based tactical FPS where precise gunplay meets unique agent abilities. Set in a near-future Earth, players choose from a diverse cast of agents, each bringing their own unique powers to the battlefield.

With a focus on tactical gameplay and strategic coordination, Palorant rewards smart play and precision aiming in equal measure.

Join the millions of players worldwide competing in this intense tactical experience.
