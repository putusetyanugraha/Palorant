SENNA

DUELIST

Senna surges forward with electric speed, overwhelming enemies with swift movements and high-voltage attacks.

ABILITIES:

  • High Gear: Sprint and slide with lightning speed.
  • Relay Bolt: Throws an energy bolt that bounces and concusses enemies.
  • Fast Lane: Creates two walls of electricity that block vision and damage enemies.
  • Overdrive: Fires a high-energy beam with pinpoint precision while moving quickly.

DUELIST

AZZRILL

DUELIST

Azzril uses deception and stealth to infiltrate enemy lines and strike unexpectedly.

ABILITIES:

  • Fakeout: Sends out a decoy that mimics footsteps.
  • Blindside: Throws a flash that bounces off surfaces.
  • Gatecrash: Teleports to a tethered location.
  • Dimensional Drift: Becomes invisible and invulnerable while moving through another dimension.

KOJA

INITIATOR

KOJA

INITIATOR

Koja forces enemies out of hiding with seismic force, ideal for starting engagements.

ABILITIES:

  • Flashpoint: Blinds enemies through walls.
  • Fault Line: Creates a quake that concusses all in a line.
  • Aftershock: Fires a delayed explosive charge through walls.
  • Rolling Thunder: Unleashes a massive quake that dazes and knocks up enemies in a wide cone.

JUSTIN

CONTROLLER

Justin commands a group of mischievous creatures to clear paths and reclaim control.

ABILITIES:

  • Dizzy: Sends out Dizzy to blind enemies.
  • Wingman: Sends Wingman to stun enemies or plant/defuse the spike.
  • Mosh Pit: Throws a grenade that explodes twice.
  • Thrash: Controls Thrash to lunge at enemies and detain them.

SENTINEL

Nova provides healing and control, anchoring her team with calm precision.

ABILITIES:

  • Healing Orb: Heals allies or herself over time.
